Customers

Customers are assigned to Groups. The supervisor is responsible for the transactions that are entered on behalf of the customers in his group. This page lists all the Customers in the group managed by a particular supervisor.

Customer ID - A unique, 13 character description that is assigned to each customer and becomes part of his user-profile.

Customer Name - The full customer name.

Remind Period Prior to Pay - The amount of time before the due date when the bank will send a reminder to the customer that the payment is due.

Edit - This icon invokes the Customer Remote page where the user can edit the number of days prior to payment.

Modifying Customers

Click on the Edit  icon on the Customers List Page. The Modify Customers Page appears. It allows the user to change the Number of Days prior to payment.

Customer ID - A unique, 13 character, description that is assigned to each customer and becomes part of his user-profile.

Customer Name - The full customer name.

Remind Period Prior to Pay - The amount of time before the due date when the bank will send a reminder to the customer that the payment is due.

Customer Local Currency - Use the drop down menu to select the customer local currency. The selected customer local currency overrides the system local currency, on any system function requiring the local currency.
