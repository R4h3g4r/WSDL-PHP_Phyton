AMEND TRANSFER LETTER OF CREDIT STEP (TAM)

The Transfer Amendment step (TAM) is used to make any amendments required to a Transfer Letter of Credit. 

Upon selection of the Amend Transfer L/C option on the Export Menu, the following Search Form is displayed:

The user can select a specific transaction by either:

  • Entering the Deal Number and/or Reference Number of the Transfer L/C that needs to be amended and clicking the Search button on the Search Form; or

  • By leaving the Deal Number and Reference Number fields empty and clicking the Search button on the Search form to display a list of relevant transactions. The user can then select a specific transaction from the list.

To view the deal, click on the deal title, which is blue and underlined. No changes can be made to the deal from the view mode.

To retrieve a Transfer L/C in order to amend it, click the Open icon next to the Deal you wish to amend. The selected deal is displayed its form completed.

Clicking on any heading in the navigation toolbar from anywhere in the application form will bring you to that section. There are three sections that must be completed in order to amend a TFR:

  • General Details

  • Settlement Data

  • Text

Amending a Transfer L/C

General Details

To modify general instruction parameters in the General Details section, select a field and enter the new information or select from a drop-down list.

The following list describes the fields for existing Transfer L/C deals. Some of these fields are mandatory and these are indicated in Bold on the screen and by this icon  in this User Guide.

    Master L/C Number - The reference number of the original transferable Letter of Credit.

Deal Number - The IMEX Deal number

    Transfer L/C No. - The number of the specific transfer step being amended.

Transfer Available Balance - The balance of the L/C available for transfer. This value is calculated and defaulted by the system

Note: The values in the fields above are defaulted from the existing Letter of Credit Deal.

Increased By - The amount by which the Transfer L/C is increased.

Decreased By - The amount by which the Transfer L/C is decreased.

Currency - The currency of the Letter of Credit.

    Amount terms - Select the relevant amount terms applicable to the Transfer L/C. If vary amount/percent is selected, enter one of the following fields:

  • Varied Amount Sum

  • Varied Amount %

    Expiry Date - Enter the date on which the Transfer L/C will expire.

Settlement Data

The second part of the Amend Transfer L/C application form contains the Settlement Data. Scroll down from the General Details to access this section.

You may also access the Settlement Data section from anywhere in the application form. Clicking the Settlement Data title on any heading of the Navigation Toolbar that precedes each section will bring you to this section.

The second part of the TAM application form contains the Settlement Data information of the TAM and the following information is required:

Days for Presentation - Specifies the number of days following the date of the transportation documents that the documents can be presented for payment.

    Available With - Specifies which bank is authorized to negotiate the documents on behalf of the beneficiary. 

Settlement By - The method of settlement is defaulted according to the orginal L/C and this field cannot be changed.

The following shipment information may be entered:

Earliest - The earliest permitted date for shipping

Latest - The latest permitted date for shipping

Texts

The Text section, which is the last part of the Transfer Letter of Credit application form, allows the user to enter additional customized information and requirements to the Letter of Credit. Scroll down from the Terms section to access these fields.

You may also access the Texts section from anywhere in the application form. Clicking on the Text title on any heading of the yellow Navigation Toolbar that precedes each section will bring you to this section.

The Text section enables you to:

  • Enter free text to specific categories

  • View and specify account details

Additional information can be entered for the following categories and may include other custom-defined categories:

Comments - Used to enter additional comments.

Customer Comments - Used to enter additional customer instructions. 

Zoom Text Icon  - Click on this icon to enlarge the text box area. You may edit or enter new data in the enlarged text box.
