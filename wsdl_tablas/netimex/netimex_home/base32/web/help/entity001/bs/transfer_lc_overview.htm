TRANSFER LETTER OF CREDIT

Establishing a New Transfer Letter of Credit

In order to establish a New Transfer Letter of Credit step (TFR), a transferable Letter of Credit deal must exist in IMEX. To establish a new TFR step, click on the Transfer L/C link on the Export Main Menu. The Transfer L/C search form appears.  Enter the relevant Deal or Reference number or click Search to display a list of transferable L/C deals in the system.

There are five sections that must be completed in order to establish a new L/C.

  • General Details

  • Settlement Data

  • Participants

  • Terms

  • Text

To sign the completed deal, click the Sign icon and enter your password.
