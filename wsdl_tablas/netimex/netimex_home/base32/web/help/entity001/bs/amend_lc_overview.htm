Amend Letter of Credit

Overview of Amending an Existing L/C

Customer-initiated L/Cs, which have been transmitted to the Trade Finance department and are subsequently issued by the bank, are listed in the Amend L/C Window. 

Upon selection of the Amend Letter of Credit option on the Import menu the following Search Form is displayed.

The user can select a specific transaction by either:

  • Entering the Deal Number and/or Reference Number of the L/C that needs to be amended and clicking on the search button on the Search Form; or
  • By leaving the Deal Number and Reference Number fields empty and clicking on the search button on the Search Form and selecting a specific transaction from the list of existing L/Cs that are displayed on the Amend Letter of Credit Window.

To view the deal, click on the deal title which is blue and underlined. No changes can be made to the deal from the view mode.

To retrieve a Letter of Credit in order to amend it, click on the OPEN Icon  next to the deal you wish to amend. The selected deal opens with its application form completed.
