AMEND STANDBY L/C

Overview of Amending a Standby L/C

Amending a Standby Letter of Credit is used to change or update information that was previously established.  The Standby L/Cs are listed in the Amend Standby L/C window. An application can be updated until it has been sent to the Bank. After that it must be amended.

Upon selection of the Amend Standby L/C option on the Standby/Guarantee menu the following Search Form is displayed.

The user can select a specific transaction by either:

  •  Entering the Deal Number and/or Reference Number of the Standby L/C that needs to be amended and clicking on the search button on the Search Form; or

  • By leaving the Deal Number and Reference Number fields empty and clicking on the search button on the Search Form and selecting a specific transaction from the list of existing Standby L/C Applications that are displayed on the Amend Standby L/C Window.

To view the deal, click on the deal title which is blue and underlined. No changes can be made to the deal from the view mode.

To retrieve a Standby L/C in order to amend it, click on the OPEN Icon next to the deal you wish to amend. The selected deal opens with its application form completed.
